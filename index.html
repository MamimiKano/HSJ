Mikvau is your one-stop shop for building materials for projects BIG and small! Whether you are building a home or adding to your current one, you can find a great selection of building materials for your project, including lumber and boards; trusses, I-joists, and engineered lumber; and concrete, cement, and masonry.
Ensure that your home is weatherproof and energy efficient by building your walls with quality insulation, panel products, and metal framing. Shop roofing to find everything you need to ensure that you have a strong roof that can effectively divert runoff caused by rain and melting snow. We offer protective and attractive siding that will give your home added curb appeal.

Add the finishing touches to the interior of your home with our selection of ceilings, drywall, and moulding. Stay organized by designating a place for each item in your home with shelves and shelving units. We have staircase systems and parts that will make your stairs an elegant part of your home. Ensure your guests can safely access your home with wheelchair ramps. Incorporate a beautiful landscaping project to your home with landscaping materials and glass blocks and glass block panels.

You can create the perfect backyard hideaway with a variety of fencing materials. Add a beautiful deck to your home with our selection of outdoor lumber and decking and deck products. If you're building a porch, add posts and columns to complete the look. Carports and shelters are great for protecting your car from the elements. Keep your tools and equipment neatly stored and out of sight with our selection of sheds, outdoor storage, and accessories. You can create the perfect area to work on your projects with work supports and workbenches.

Whether you are going to be building a home or garage, we have the books and building plans to help you plan and build your project. Choose from our collection of pole barn/post frame buildings, or design your own custom building to fit your needs.
